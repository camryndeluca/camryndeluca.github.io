    • As a child, I consumed an abnormal amount of internet and pop culture. Having a sister who was eight years older exposed me to MTV reality television in its heyday and all the greatest hits of the early to mid-2000s. Rather than droning on to my loved ones about pertinent moments in pop culture, I've decided to put it to good use.
